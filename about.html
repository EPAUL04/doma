About Doma

Doma is a website made to allow users to practice flashcard sets. It was created with a focus on language learning in mind. The name comes from the Spanish word "idioma" which means "language". The logo is stylized by replacing "a" with "ə", which is the symbol in the International Phonetic Alphabet for the "uh" sound at the end of the word. It is also enclosed in slashes, because these slashes are used to transcribe things in IPA. I wanted to incorporate references to language learning and linguistics throughout, since those are interests of mine which inspired this project.
