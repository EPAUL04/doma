Domə is a website made to allow users to practice flashcard sets. It was created with a focus of language learning in mind. The name comes from the Spanish word "idioma" which means "language" and is stylized with a "ə", which is the symbol in the International Phonetic Alphabet for the "uh" sound at the end of the word. I wanted to incorporate references to language learning and linguistics throughout, since those are interests of mine which inspired this project.
