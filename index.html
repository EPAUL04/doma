Welcome to Domə!

Domə is a flashcard website aimed at practicing vocabulary while learning a language! Here's how to use it:

  1. First, fill out your dictionary to store your terms and translations/definitions, or head over to the library to find existing sets you would like to practice!
  2. Second, head over to the Flashcards section to get started!
  3. Enjoy!

As a note, this website will not actually teach you a language, it is just for practice!
